How to use SnipLocal

  1. 1
    Drop or click to load a JPG, PNG, or WebP image.
  2. 2
    Click Remove background — the model runs entirely in your browser. Nothing is uploaded.
  3. 3
    Adjust settings — result updates instantly, no re-run needed.
    Background — transparent, solid colour, or custom pick Soften edges — feathers the mask for hair & fine detail Passport — crops to country spec, white background applied
  4. 4
    Download PNG — or use 4-up print sheet to get 4 passport photos on A4.
